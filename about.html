Mila Lin Tabach

I am a senior at Columbia University, double majoring in Computer Science and Film, with a strong interest in how technology, design, and storytelling come together to shape user experiences. My coursework follows an applied computer science track, with a focus on artificial intelligence, machine learning, databases, and user interface design.

I have conducted HCI research at Barnard's Design Tools Lab, exploring how people interact with creative and technical systems and contributing to user-centered design research. Outside the classroom, I have interned in software development and consulting, gaining experience across technical, product, and strategy teams. In parallel, I have directed student films and theatrical productions, strengthening my skills in leadership, collaboration, and creative problem-solving.

I am seeking roles in Software Development, UI/UX design, UX research, product management, or consulting, where I can bridge technical rigor and creative thinking to build thoughtful, user-focused products in collaborative environments.
